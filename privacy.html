Privacy Policy

Home / Privacy Policy

1. Information We Collect

At AFB Trade & Services Inc., your privacy is paramount. When you register for our portal or utilize our contact forms, we securely collect information such as your name, email address, phone number, and areas of primary business interest. During active consultancy, we may securely process additional legal or financial documents necessary for immigration or real estate transactions.

2. How We Use Your Data

The data collected is used strictly to provide you with tailored consultancy services, secure access to your account portal, and to communicate important updates regarding your files. We do not sell your personal data to any external marketing agencies.

3. Data Security and Infrastructure

We employ enterprise-grade security architecture. Your account credentials and data are securely handled via Supabase, utilizing rigorous encryption protocols. All network traffic is forced over HTTPS and proxied through modern Web Application Firewalls.

4. Third-Party Sharing

We only share data with third parties when it is strictly necessary to fulfill your service requirements (e.g., submitting documentation to Canadian border and immigration authorities, or coordinating with global shipping logistics partners). All partners are bound by strict confidentiality agreements.

5. Your Rights

You reserve the right to request a full export of the personal data we hold about you, or request its deletion subject to Canadian federal data retention laws regarding financial and immigration records. To exercise these rights, please contact info@afbtrade.ca.
